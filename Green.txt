Green
Green is a color deeply intertwined with nature and growth. From the vibrant green of new spring leaves to the deep emerald of a lush forest, green evokes feelings of tranquility and renewal. It represents life, fertility, and harmony. Green can symbolize hope and optimism, as in the phrase "green shoots of recovery." It can also be associated with envy or jealousy, as in the expression "green with envy." Different shades of green have their own distinct character. Lime green is bright and energetic, while forest green is calming and serene. Green is a color that connects us to the natural world and reminds us of the cycles of life.
